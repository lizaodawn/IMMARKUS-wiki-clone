Working with [IIIF](https://iiif.io/get-started/why-iiif/#:~:text=IIIF%20is%20a%20set%20of%20open%20standards%20for,backed%20by%20a%20consortium%20of%20leading%20cultural%20institutions) offers a lightweight and interoperable solution for accessing high-quality images. Thanks to its shared framework for sourcing and sharing, you can explore a variety of collections—maps, drawings, archival documents, printed books, and more.
